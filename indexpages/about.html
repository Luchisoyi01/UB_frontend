History

Uzuri Bank was founded by a group of visionary farmers who recognized the need for a financial institution that truly understands the agricultural community. Established in 2017, Uzuri Bank has grown from a small cooperative to a trusted financial partner for farmers and rural communities across the region and the country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers and rural communities by providing accessible, reliable, and innovative financial services. We are committed to supporting the agricultural sector and fostering sustainable growth.

Our Values

At Uzuri Bank, our values are the foundation of everything we do. They guide our actions, shape our culture, and reflect our commitment to our customers and communities.
